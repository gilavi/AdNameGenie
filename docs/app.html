⚠️

macOS may block the app the first time you open it, saying "Apple could not verify…". This is normal for apps distributed outside the App Store. Here's how to fix it:

1
Drag NameCraft to Applications Open the DMG and drag the app into your Applications folder
2
Right-click the app → Open Don't double-click — right-click instead, choose Open, then click "Open" in the dialog
OR use Terminal (one-time)
xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/NameCraft.app

Paste this in Terminal after installing. After that it opens normally, no warnings ever again.
